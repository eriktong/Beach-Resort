Frequently Asked Questions

Seraphim Beach Resort

What are your current Staycation Guidlines
Only fully vaccinated guests will be granted access to the property. Minors must be accompanied by fully vaccinated parents or guardians. Please be advised that guidelines for staycation are subject to change without prior notice. For more details, you may call us at +63 (2) 8888 0777
I have a room reservation at Seraphim Beach Resort. Can I bring food and alcoholic beverage in your resort
For the safety and wellbeing of our guests, kindly be advised that food delivery services will not be allowed inside the property including bringing of food from outside the resort. Alternatively, guests may order directly from in-room dining. Alcoholic beverages, on the other hand, may still be permitted and for security clearance, a maximum allowable quantity is being implemented. Moreover, to ensure that all our guests will have an extraordinary experience, partying inside the room is strongly discouraged. Kindly email us to know more about our suite packages.
I'm staying with a friend/family member who has a room reservation. Do you allow visitors in the room?
Visitors of in-house guests may also enjoy and experience the room from 12 noon until 10 PM only. Also, there's a limit as to how many visitors we may allow depending on your room type. Only fully vaccinated visitors with vaccination cards presented will be permitted. If visitors will stay beyond the given visiting time, corresponding extra charges may apply. Moreover, to ensure that all our guests will have an extraordinary experience, we discourage partying inside the room.
Do you allow pets in your resort?
We only allow service pets with proper documentation on the premises of the property. Service pets are the animals who have documentation and are capable of assisting physically challenged guests.
