Seraphim Beach Resort

Beach Resort is located on one of the best beaches in Asia. An unmissable stay for your next holiday in paradise!

Room Booking

Seraphim Beach Resort As comfortable as being at home.

Seraphim Beach is an incredible 4 kilometers long and is known for the soft touch of its fine powder like white sand. Looking down from high above, you can see another beautiful beach connected to Seraphim beach called is Calitang Beach. This connected beach lies just South of Seraphim Beach and is easily accessible by foot. Climb up the small hill where the two beaches meet for absolutely stunning views the two twin beaches and the nearby Lalutaya Island.

Walking to the beach

Seraphim Beach Resort is just meters from the beautiful 4km long Seraphim Beach. Feel the peace of the sea breeze while going for long afternoon walks on the beach. Have dinner under the most incredible sunset seascape scene in the World with restaurants directly on the beach.

Sun bathing

Seraphim Beach Resort offers a variety of facilities to enrich your stay in El Nido, including access to a private swimming pool, gardens, sun loungers on the beach and a nearby restaurant bar just meters from the rooms!

Local cuisine

In addition to local Filipino cuisine, El Nido also has a fantastic selection of beach bar restaurants, offering menus from Japan, China, Europe and America.
