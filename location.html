Location

Seraphim Beach Resort

Heart of El Nido

The nearest airport to Seraphim Beach Resort is El Nido Airport located approximately 30 minutes drive away. Puerto Princesa also has an airport (Note: It takes 5 hours to drive from Puerto Princesa to El Nido town). Seraphim Beach Resort is located on Seraphim Beach, which is about 45 minutes North of El Nido Town by car (approx. 22 km).

How to get to Seraphim Beach Resort

The shuttle bus will take you directly to the entrance of Seraphim Beach Resort located just behind the popular Sunmai Bar and Restaurant. Seraphim Beach Resort does not provide airport transfers and must be arranged separately.

We advise guests not to take a tuk tuk or tricycle to Seraphim Beach Resort as some sections of the road are not yet paved and can be extremely uncomfortable, especially during the rainy season. If you have your own transport, simply navigate to the Sunmai Bar and Restaurant, where you will find parking for the resort at the back.Once there, let the friendly staff know that you are staying at the Seraphim Beach Resort and they will assist you from there.
